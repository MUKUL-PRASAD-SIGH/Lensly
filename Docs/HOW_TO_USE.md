# How to Use ValueLens on Your Phone

---

## Installation

1. Download ValueLens from Google Play Store
2. Open the app
3. Follow the 2-step setup (takes about 1 minute)

---

## Setup — Step by Step

### Step 1: Enable Screen Reading

ValueLens needs to read product information from shopping apps.

1. Tap **"Enable Screen Reading"** in the app
2. You'll be taken to your phone's Accessibility Settings
3. Find **ValueLens** in the list and tap it
4. Toggle it **ON**
5. Read the confirmation and tap **Allow**
6. Press the back button to return to ValueLens

What this does: lets ValueLens read product names, prices, and weights from Zepto, Blinkit, and Instamart. It does not read your messages, banking apps, or passwords.

### Step 2: Enable Overlay

ValueLens needs to show a small button on top of your shopping app.

1. Tap **"Allow Display Over Apps"** in the app
2. You'll be taken to Settings
3. Find ValueLens and toggle **"Allow display over other apps"** ON
4. Return to ValueLens

### Done

You'll see a confirmation screen. ValueLens is ready.

---

## Activating ValueLens While Shopping

Open any supported shopping app (Zepto, Blinkit, Instamart, Amazon).

You'll see a small **ValueLens bubble** floating on the right edge of the screen.

**To open the panel:** Tap the bubble.

The panel slides in from the side. Your shopping app is still visible and usable behind the panel.

---

## Asking a Question

Once the panel is open, type your question in the search bar at the bottom of the panel.

**Examples to try:**

For toothpaste:
- `best price per gram`
- `which one is actually cheapest`
- `healthiest option`
- `is this discount real`

For protein powder:
- `best protein per rupee`
- `highest protein under ₹800`
- `lowest sugar`

For snacks / cereal:
- `lowest calories per rupee`
- `avoid palm oil`
- `best value family pack`

For any category:
- `best overall`
- `which lasts longest`
- `worth the extra cost?`

Tap the arrow or press Enter. Results appear in 1–2 seconds.

---

## Reading the Results

The result panel shows ranked product cards.

Each card shows:
- **Rank** (1 = best for your query)
- **Product name**
- **Key metric** (e.g., ₹0.56/gram)
- **One-line reason** (e.g., "Cheapest per gram. No misleading discount.")

**Tap "Why?"** on any card for a full explanation.

**Tap "Compare"** to see a side-by-side breakdown of the top 2–3 options.

---

## Fake Discount Warning

If ValueLens detects that a "discount" does not actually make a product cheaper per unit than alternatives, you'll see a yellow warning:

> ⚠️ Discount may be misleading — effective price is higher per gram than Pack B

This is based on the effective unit price calculation, not the percentage shown.

---

## Dismissing the Panel

- Tap anywhere outside the panel
- Or tap the **×** in the panel header

The panel closes and you return to exactly where you were in the shopping app.

## Quick Tips

**Scroll first, then ask.** If you're on a page with many products, scroll down a bit to load more before activating ValueLens. It reads what's visible on the screen.

**Use the sort feature in the app first.** If you sort by price in Zepto before activating ValueLens, it can work even faster because fewer products need to be analyzed.

**Be specific in your query.** "Best value" works. "Best value toothpaste for sensitive teeth under ₹200" works better.

**Check the metric shown.** For liquids, the key metric is ₹/ml. For food, it's ₹/gram or protein/₹. The card tells you which metric was used for ranking.

---

## Turning ValueLens Off

To pause ValueLens temporarily:
- Long-press the floating bubble → tap **Pause**
- The bubble disappears until you reopen the app

To disable permanently:
- Go to your phone's **Settings → Accessibility → ValueLens** → toggle off

---

## Privacy

ValueLens does not:
- Store screenshots
- Record your screen
- Send raw product text to any server
- Access your banking, messages, or photos

All your preferences and query history stay on your phone, encrypted.

You can delete all stored data at any time from **ValueLens Settings → Clear All Data**.
